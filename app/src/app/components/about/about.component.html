Stream Ripper Subscription Service

By providing an Url and Access Token, this website rips the stream or partitions the stream into .mp3 file and subsequently uploads the music to a service (or "sink") you provided. You can also listen to them via your browser.

You can use ShoutCast to find an online radio and use it's url.

This service is free, I do NOT own the ripped .mp3 files and I do NOT store them, only upload them from your browser into your file sharing service

Why I created this

I actually don't like listening to music but I have always been curious of ShoutCast streams and online radios. So why not.
